Eid-ul-Fitr is one of the most significant and joyous religious celebrations in Pakistan and across the Muslim world. It marks the end of Ramadan, the month of fasting, and is a time for family, community, and gratitude

Eid-ul-Adha, also known as the Festival of Sacrifice, is one of the most important religious holidays in Islam, celebrated by Muslims worldwide, including in Pakistan. This festival marks the culmination of the Hajj pilgrimage and commemorates the willingness of the Prophet Ibrahim (Abraham) to sacrifice his son Isma'il in obedience to God's command.

Ramadan is the ninth month of the Islamic lunar calendar and is one of the most important and revered months for Muslims around the world, including in Pakistan. It is a time of fasting, prayer, reflection, and community.
